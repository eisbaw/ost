# Opus - Opus Audio Codec

Open, royalty-free audio codec standardized by IETF (RFC 6716). Handles both speech and music well.

## Features

- Variable bitrate: 6 kbps to 510 kbps
- Sample rates: 8 kHz to 48 kHz
- Low latency (down to 2.5 ms)
- In-band FEC
- DTX support

## Teams Usage

Opus is compiled into Teams but SILK is the primary production codec.
