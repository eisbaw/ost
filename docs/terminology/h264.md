# H.264 - Advanced Video Coding (AVC)

ITU-T/ISO video compression standard, widely used for video conferencing and streaming.

## Profiles

- **Baseline** - Low complexity, no B-frames
- **Constrained Baseline** - Subset for video conferencing
- **Main** - B-frames, CABAC
- **High** - 8x8 transforms, additional features

## Teams Note

Standard H.264 is typically NOT accepted by Teams infrastructure. Teams uses [X-H264UC](x-h264uc.md), a proprietary variant.
